1. PURPOSE
The purpose of this Agreement is to establish the terms under which the Participant provides a Refundable Performance / Commitment Fee (the “Fee”) to the Company as an indication of intent, seriousness, and commitment towards participation in specific tasks, projects, or tenders managed by the Company.
This Fee is not a security investment, shareholding, or financial instrument under any securities regulation. It is strictly a refundable performance-based deposit subject to the conditions stated herein.
________________________________________
2. NATURE OF THE FEE
The Fee shall be:
●	Paid voluntarily by the Participant to confirm their willingness to engage and complete agreed-upon tasks and or job sets.

●	Treated as a Performance/Commitment Fee, not as capital investment, loan, or equity.

●	Held by the Company for the duration of the Participant’s active engagement and performance indications.

________________________________________
3. REFUND CONDITIONS
The Fee shall be refunded to the Participant as follows:
1.	Full Refund: Upon satisfactory completion of the assigned tasks, deliverables, or project milestones as verified by the Company's verification structure process.

2.	Partial Refund: If the Participant has completed a significant portion of the work but has failed to fully meet all requirements; the refundable amount will be determined proportionally based on completion percentage and contribution value.

3.	Non-Refundable Portion: The Fee (or a part thereof) shall be forfeited if the Participant:

○	Fails to complete the assigned tasks within the specified time frame;

○	Demonstrates non-cooperation, negligence, or unprofessional conduct;

○	Violates the terms of engagement or confidentiality obligations.

○	Violates the terms of NDA, in which case the Fee shall be immediately forfeited (non-refundable), and the Company reserves the right to pursue all available legal remedies.

________________________________________
4. INCENTIVE STRUCTURE
The Company may provide additional monetary or non-monetary incentives upon successful task fulfillment. These incentives may include performance bonuses, early completion rewards, or reputation points within the Company’s ecosystem.
Refunds and incentives may be distributed gradually or in stages, based on the project’s progress and blockchain-recorded task validation (if applicable).
________________________________________
5. USE OF FUNDS
The Participant agrees that during the performance period:
●	The Fee will remain refundable under the terms stated above, and the Company guarantees refund eligibility upon fulfillment of agreed performance standards.

●	The Participant agrees that during the performance period, the Fee may be temporarily utilized as part of the Company’s operational liquidity and cash flow management. The Company guarantees that the Fee remains refundable to the Participant upon fulfillment of the agreed performance standards and conditions set forth in this Agreement.

________________________________________

The Parties acknowledge and agree that:
●	The Fee does not constitute a security, investment, or ownership interest under the U.S. Securities Act of 1933 or similar international regulations.

●	There is no expectation of profit derived from the Company’s operations.

●	The Fee exists solely as a refundable commitment for ensuring task performance and engagement discipline.

________________________________________
7. TERMINATION
Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice under the following conditions:
●	If the Participant withdraws voluntarily before project assignment (in which case, the Fee may be refunded minus administrative charges) within next 21 working days.

●	If the Company discontinues the relevant project or substantially changes its operational scope before the assignment begins, the Company shall provide a full refund of the Fee within 30 business days or as per announcement of full refund(s) disclosure acknowledgement.

Upon termination, the Company will refund the eligible and or complete portion of the Fee within 30 business days from the official termination date.
